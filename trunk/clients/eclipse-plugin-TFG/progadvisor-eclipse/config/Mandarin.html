Mandarin and ginger biscuits

por Encar, March 15, 2012 12:33 pm

Ingredients

  • 500 gr. pastry flour
  • 1 cdta. baking powder
  • 2 cdtas. ground ginger
  • From scratch 1 large tangerine skin
  • 150 gr. tangerine juice
  • 150 gr. de Azucar glass
  • 150 gr. olive oil
  • 1 yolk

Although spring has not arrived, the atmosphere is absolutely spring, warm and very pleasant. Makes you want to enjoy it and go with the joy that passes this time… but for how bad it is proving a lack of rain for agriculture in our country at this time. So looking forward to change the time and rain arrives, I propose these delicious cookies. Hopefully you can enjoy the house watching the water fall through the glass.

Elaboration

  1. In a bowl mix the juice, oil, egg yolk and the scratch of Mandarin, with rods, we beat it well until all is mixed.
  2. In another bowl mix the flour, sugar, yeast and ginger.
  3. Little by little we are laying the dry ingredients into the bowl of liquids while constantly stir rods. When we reach a point where the mass is consistent and had to knead with hands until we have left a homogeneous wet.
  4. Cover of plastic wrap and let dough rest in the fridge, at least, 1 time.
  5. With a wooden roller extend the dough, leaving a 2 mm. thick, with a pastry cutter, cookies and do we putting on a baking sheet covered with parchment paper. As we leave a 20 cookies we prepare 3 hornearemos plates and in 3 times.
  6. Boil the biscuits, in preheated oven at 180 º C, about 15 minutes.
